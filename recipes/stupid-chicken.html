Stupid Chicken

5-Ingredient Stupid Chicken is an Italian chicken recipe that could not be simpler to make. This do-it-in-your-sleep recipe is a must have. If you're looking for a way to get dinner on the table fast after a long day, this is your recipe. This recipe doesn't require a trip to the store either since most of these items you may already have at home. Chicken breasts, diced tomatoes, garlic and herb dry soup mix, Parmesan cheese, and pasta or rice are all you need! Throw your ingredients in a slow cooker before you leave in the morning and by the time you get back home and are ready to eat, this dish will be ready and waiting for you!

Ingredients

  • 4 skinless boneless chicken breasts
  • 1 (28-ounce) can Italian-seasoned diced tomatoes
  • 1 envelope dry herb and garlic soup mix
  • hot cooked rice or pasta
  • Parmesan cheese, grated

Directions

  1. Cut chicken into bite-size pieces and throw into the bottom of your slow cooker. Add in the diced tomatoes and herb and garlic soup mix, and mix to combine.
  2. Cook on LOW for 6 to 8 hours, until chicken is cooked through and tender
  3. In the meantime, cook your pasta or rice. When chicken is done cooking, serve it over pasta or rice and sprinkle with Parmesan cheese.
